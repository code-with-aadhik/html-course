INTERNATIONAL NEWS

World leaders, climate experts, and environmental activists gathered this week for a high-level Global Climate Summit aimed at addressing the growing challenges of climate change. The summit brought together representatives from over 150 countries to discuss solutions focused on renewable energy, emission reductions, and sustainable development.

World leaders gathering in Brazil

The event comes amid rising global temperatures, extreme weather events, and increasing public demand for stronger environmental action. Leaders described the summit as a “critical turning point” for international climate cooperation.
